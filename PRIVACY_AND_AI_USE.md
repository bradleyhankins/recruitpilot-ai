# Privacy and AI Use

RecruitPilot AI is a public portfolio demo and responsible ATS Lite workflow assistant. It is designed for fictional, sample, or non-sensitive resume review scenarios.

## User Data Guidance

Do not upload or enter sensitive, confidential, regulated, or private information into the public demo.

Avoid uploading or entering:

- Real candidate personally identifiable information
- Social Security numbers or government IDs
- Date of birth or age-related details
- Medical, disability, immigration, or protected-class information
- Compensation, payroll, or background-check information
- Internal company secrets
- Passwords, API keys, or credentials

## AI Processing

When an OpenAI token is configured, selected job description and resume text may be sent to the AI provider to improve the human-review summary, follow-up questions, and candidate communication.

The app is built so that:

- Rules-based review organization is the source of truth
- AI only improves wording, clarity, and structure
- AI supports human review preparation only
- AI should not rank applicants or make selection/rejection decisions
- AI should not infer or evaluate protected traits
- AI failure falls back silently to rules-based output
- The app does not intentionally store uploaded resume text

## Responsible Use

RecruitPilot AI should not be used as the sole basis for selection, rejection, compensation, or employment decisions.

Human judgment and compliant hiring practices are required.

## Upload Guidance

Use fictional, anonymized, or generalized resume text for the public demo.

## Input Limits

The AI helper trims long prompts before AI enhancement to improve reliability and control cost.

Future hardening should include file size limits and text length limits for resume uploads.

## Public Demo Note

All built-in sample names, companies, and scenarios are fictional and created for portfolio demonstration purposes.
