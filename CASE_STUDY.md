# RecruitPilot AI Case Study

## Overview

RecruitPilot AI is an AI-assisted candidate screening workflow tool for field-sales and small-business teams. It helps hiring managers turn candidate details and interview notes into fit scores, risk levels, green flags, red flags, follow-up questions, interview scorecards, hiring recommendations, candidate emails, onboarding plans, and downloadable candidate review reports.

## Problem

Small and mid-sized businesses often do not have structured recruiting systems. Hiring decisions may be based on scattered notes, informal interviews, inconsistent questions, and manager memory.

This creates common problems:

- Candidate evaluations are hard to compare
- Important red flags may be missed
- Interview follow-up questions are inconsistent
- Hiring recommendations are not clearly documented
- Onboarding focus areas are not identified early
- Managers lack a repeatable screening process

## Solution

RecruitPilot AI creates a structured workflow for candidate screening.

A user enters candidate-safe information, including:

- Role applied for
- Sales experience
- Industry experience
- Prospecting comfort
- CRM experience
- Compensation alignment
- Availability
- Communication rating
- Coachability rating
- Reliability rating
- Interview notes

The app then generates practical hiring outputs that a manager can immediately use.

## Business Impact

RecruitPilot AI demonstrates how lightweight AI-assisted workflows can improve hiring consistency without requiring enterprise HR software.

Potential benefits include:

- More consistent candidate evaluation
- Better interview preparation
- Clearer hiring recommendations
- Stronger manager documentation
- Faster identification of candidate risks
- Earlier onboarding planning
- More repeatable recruiting operations processes
